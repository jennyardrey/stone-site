Welcome to Stone Management Group

Stone Management Group Limited is a security company founded in 1998 and located in Liverpool. The business provides security officers, CCTV Surveillance and Key Holding services to, principally, commercial, business and residential buildings, retail and business parks, event venues, construction sites and other clients. The business targets building management companies and large retail stores in order to establish a strong base of clients in the North West of England

Our portfolio is expansive throughout the North of England, with sites ranging from residential and commercial office blocks to freight depots, schools and shopping centres.

Each of our sites benefits from SMG's professional and personal service. We pride ourselves on providing an unrivalled level of customer service and the ability to maintain the highest of standards, with a level of consistency and care rarely seen in the industry.

Whilst our range of services are market leaders in terms of quality, it is when integrated into a client's building management plan that our impeccably high Stone Standard can be fully appreciated and of true benefit to our clients.

Stone Management Group holds Approved Contractor Status for the provision of Security Guarding and Key Holding services and Public Space Surveillance (CCTV) services.
